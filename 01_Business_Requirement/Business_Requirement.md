# Customer Churn Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior to identify churned customers, understand churn patterns, and provide business recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Business Problem
Many businesses lose customers without understanding why. Customer churn directly impacts revenue and profitability. The goal is to identify churned customers and the factors contributing to churn.

## Objectives
- Identify churned customers.
- Analyze customer purchase behavior.
- Segment customers based on activity.
- Discover key churn drivers.
- Provide actionable business recommendations.

## KPIs
- Total Customers
- Active Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate (%)
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Repeat Purchase Rate
